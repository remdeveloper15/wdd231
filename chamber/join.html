NP Membership Benefits

  • No membership fee for qualifying non-profit organizations.
  • Listing in the chamber online directory.
  • Access to basic networking events.
  • Monthly newsletter with community updates.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • All NP benefits.
  • Discounted tickets for chamber events and workshops.
  • Social media mentions 1x per quarter.
  • Basic advertising in chamber newsletters.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Featured placement in the business directory.
  • Invitation to member-only trainings and roundtables.
  • Event sponsorship opportunities with discounts.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits.
  • Homepage spotlight advertising rotations.
  • Priority access to chamber-led campaigns and press.
  • VIP access and recognition at major chamber events.
